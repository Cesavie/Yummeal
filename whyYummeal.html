It's not just preparing healthy meal... it's also eating deliciously.
We simplify the meal-planning process. Our tools equip households to eat better food, eat together, save money at the grocery store, and have a less stressful cooking experience.
Our Story. Yummeal was born from our desire to make the meal planning process simpler. We realized that it could benefit families and individuals who are always busy. Read more about The Yummeal Story and what inspired us to create a digital meal planning program.
Why we do it. Our physical and emotional health is directly tied to what we eat, and the best way to ensure we are getting the highest quality food is to prepare it at home. Without a system to organize recipes, plan menus, and compile shopping lists, our kitchens will lack the ingredients we need to prepare our meals. Our hope is that Yummeal will equip you to prepare delicious, wholesome food at home that nourishes both body and soul.
Who we do it for. Yummeal is built for all the fitness lover who wants a curated meal. Other meal planning services will hand you a pre-made menu and shopping list that uses their recipes and send you on your way. That's fine for some, but it can never be perfect for you. With Yummeal you can be assured of a well-balanced meal that fit your lifestyle, design your health goal, then go to the store with a list organized the way you like to shop. That's the beauty of doing it yourself.
